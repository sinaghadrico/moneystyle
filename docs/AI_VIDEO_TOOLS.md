# AI Video Tools for MoneyStyle Marketing

Guide for creating marketing videos using AI tools. No video editing skills needed.

---

## 1. URL-to-Video (Give URL, Get Video)

### Creatify
- **URL:** https://creatify.ai
- **How:** Paste `moneystyle.app` → AI creates ad video with avatar, voiceover, and screenshots
- **Best for:** Social media ads (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)
- **Price:** $29/mo (10 videos)
- **Output:** 15-60 sec vertical/horizontal video

### Shuffll
- **URL:** https://shuffll.com
- **How:** Give URL + brand guidelines → AI generates marketing video
- **Best for:** Professional product videos, LinkedIn
- **Price:** $49/mo

---

## 2. Prompt-to-Video (Describe What You Want)

### Invideo AI (Recommended to Start)
- **URL:** https://ai.invideo.io
- **How:** Write a prompt, AI creates full video with stock footage, voiceover, music
- **Best for:** YouTube, social media, product demos
- **Price:** Free (with watermark) / $25/mo (no watermark)
- **Example prompt:**
  ```
  Create a 30-second social media video about MoneyStyle, a free
  AI-powered personal finance tracker at moneystyle.app. Show features
  like receipt scanning, dashboard charts, telegram bot, and savings
  goals. Upbeat tone, modern style.
  ```

### Fliki
- **URL:** https://fliki.ai
- **How:** Paste text or blog post → AI converts to video with voiceover
- **Best for:** Blog-to-video, quick social clips
- **Price:** Free (5 min/mo) / $28/mo

### Pictory
- **URL:** https://pictory.ai
- **How:** Paste blog post or script → AI creates video with captions
- **Best for:** Repurposing blog content to video
- **Price:** $23/mo

## 3. AI Avatar Videos (Virtual Presenter)

### HeyGen
- **URL:** https://heygen.com
- **How:** Choose avatar, write script → realistic AI person presents your product
- **Best for:** Product Hunt launch video, explainer videos
- **Price:** $29/mo
- **Languages:** 40+ including English, Arabic, Farsi

### Synthesia
- **URL:** https://synthesia.io
- **How:** Same as HeyGen but more professional avatars
- **Best for:** Enterprise-level product demos
- **Price:** $29/mo

### Elai
- **URL:** https://elai.io
- **How:** Write text → AI avatar presents with slides
- **Best for:** Feature walkthroughs, tutorials
- **Price:** $23/mo

---

## 4. Screen Recording + AI Edit

### Tella
- **URL:** https://tella.tv
- **How:** Record your screen → AI edits, adds zoom, trims dead space
- **Best for:** Detailed product demos (3-5 min)
- **Price:** Free (basic) / $19/mo

### Descript
- **URL:** https://descript.com
- **How:** Record → AI transcribes → edit video by editing text
- **Best for:** YouTube tutorials, podcast clips
- **Price:** Free (basic) / $24/mo
- **Killer feature:** Delete a word from transcript → AI removes it from video

---

## 5. Short-Form Video (Reels/Shorts/TikTok)

### OpusClip
- **URL:** https://opus.pro
- **How:** Upload long video → AI finds best 15-60 sec clips with captions
- **Best for:** Repurposing demos into Reels/Shorts
- **Price:** Free (10 clips/mo) / $19/mo

### Creatify (also listed above)
- **Best for:** Ad-style short videos from URL

## Recommended Workflow for MoneyStyle

### Phase 1: Launch (Free/Low Cost)
1. **Invideo AI** (free) — Create 3 social videos with different angles:
   - "This free app has 30 features" (feature overview)
   - "How to scan a receipt with AI" (feature demo)
   - "Free YNAB alternative" (comparison angle)
2. **Tella** (free) — Record 3-min full product demo

### Phase 2: Growth ($50-80/mo)
1. **HeyGen** ($29/mo) — AI avatar Product Hunt video
2. **Creatify** ($29/mo) — 10 ad videos for TikTok/Instagram
3. **OpusClip** (free) — Cut demo into Shorts

### Phase 3: Scale ($100+/mo)
1. **Synthesia** — Professional explainer series
2. **Descript** — YouTube channel with tutorials
3. **Pictory** — Convert every blog post to video

MoneyStyle is a 100% free, open-source personal finance tracker with 30+ features

savings goals, and AI-powered financial scoring.

Website: moneystyle.app
Tone: [upbeat/professional/casual]
